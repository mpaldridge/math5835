Summary:

  • A good importance sampling distribution \(Y\) is one whose PDF \(g(y)\) is roughly proportional to \(|f(y)\,\phi(y)|\). Equivalently, \(\frac{f(y)}{g(y)}|\phi(y)|\) is approximately constant.
